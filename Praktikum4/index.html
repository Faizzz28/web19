About

An Economy Hotel for Smart & Eco-Friendly Traveler

Smart Traveler

Catering for the new up-coming generation of smart travelers who know what they want and choose a hotel that corresponds to their own style and values, and also to the budget they are ready to spend for each of their different traveling purposes.

High Value

More than just a place to sleep, it is a concept, a brand perfectly fitted to the lifestyle of an emerging new social category, looking for an efficient and affordable accommodation. The younger generation and concerned by the future of their country and the environment, and addicted to Internet connection and social networks, will represent a favorite target market.

Eco-Friendly

It is aiming at becoming a vehicle promoting an eco-friendly awareness and attitude among guests, suppliers, neighbors and business partners. RAAH! Hotels will be associated to any positive initiative related to the environment awareness, locally, within their provinces or nationwide.
